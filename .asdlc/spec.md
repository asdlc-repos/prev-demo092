# Overview

An internal leave management application where WSO2 employees request time off and managers approve or reject those requests.

# Personas

- Employee — requests leave and tracks their balance.
- Manager — approves or rejects leave requests from their team.

# Features

- An employee submits a leave request for specific dates and selects a leave type.
- Managers see pending requests from their direct reports.
- A manager approves or rejects a request.
- Employees view the status of their submitted requests.
- Everyone can see their current leave balance by type.
